Chaostreff Osnabrück e.V. – All creatures welcome!

Are you interested in security, alternative operating systems, free (libre) software and network politics? Welcome to the Chaostreff Osnabrück e.V., your hub for all those who want to hack and shape the world.

Our mission: We are a group of hackers and tech geeks who meet regularly to share ideas and develop projects. Everyone is welcome, whether you are experienced or curious.

Join us! Visit our meetings and be part of our chaotic troop. We look forward to meeting you!

Digital Independence Day
Aug 2, 2026, 2:00 PM - 6:00 PM
Hackspace Rabbithole - Alte Münze 12, 49074 Osnabrück

We support and participate in the Digital Independence Day movement.

Every first Sunday of the month, we are on-site to offer support for taking steps towards digital independence.

These don't always have to be big steps. Perhaps you'd like to stop buying books or e-books from the big monopolist and instead support your local bookstore. Or you're wondering how to set up an email address with a European provider.

Feel free to drop by with your questions about digital independence. We'll provide advice based on our experience and the collected recipes from the nationwide initiative, offering help for self-help.

If you have specific topic requests, you're welcome to contact us in advance so we can potentially include them in the next or a following event.

Our Hackspace Rabbithole is located on the second floor and is unfortunately not barrier-free. If this poses a challenge for you, please contact us - we're happy to find alternatives together.

Workshop: Internet-ABC – Surf license for kids
Sep 21, 2026, 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM
Lagerhalle - Rolandsmauer 26, 49074 Osnabrück

Test your knowledge about the internet with the surfschein quiz by internet ABC — get your "drivers license for the internet" at digital week 2025!

The surfschein is a playful digital learning quiz. It provides a protected environment for kids, ages 7 to 12, to learn the basic skills for responsible and self determined interaction with the internet.

Because the quiz is not translated, this workshop will be held in German only.

Registration opens around August 15 on the Osnabrück Digital Week website

Digital Independence Day – wieso, weshalb, warum?
Sep 24, 2026, 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM
Lagerhalle - Rolandsmauer 26, 49074 Osnabrück
Speaker: Nils Israel

Democracy needs open spaces. Who decides what information is visible and shared online? Who determines which internet services can be used by whom? Do I really own my data? Is there anything I can do differently myself?

Brought to the forefront by Marc-Uwe Kling and the Kangaroo at the 39th Chaos Communication Congress in December 2025 in Hamburg, a great many people throughout Germany and beyond have since been getting involved under the banner: Digital Independence Day.

This talk explains exactly what this is all about. It aims to provide strategies for becoming more independent and self-determined, even in small steps.

The talk will be held in German.

Registration opens around August 15 on the Osnabrück Digital Week website
