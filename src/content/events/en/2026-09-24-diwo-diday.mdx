Democracy needs open spaces.
Who decides what information is visible and shared online?
Who determines which internet services can be used by whom?
Do I really own my data?
Is there anything I can do differently myself?

Brought to the forefront by Marc-Uwe Kling and the Kangaroo at the 39th Chaos Communication Congress in December 2025
in Hamburg, a great many people throughout Germany
and beyond have since been getting involved under the banner: Digital Independence Day.

This talk explains exactly what this is all about. It aims to provide strategies for
becoming more independent and self-determined, even in small steps.

The talk will be held in German.

Registration opens around August 15 on the [Osnabrück Digital Week website](https://digitalewoche-osnabrueck.de)
