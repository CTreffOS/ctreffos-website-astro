Just like in the past years, we will be offering some workshops as part of Osnabrücks digital festival Digitale Woche 2026.

The programme will be published on [digitalewoche-osnabrueck.de](https://digitalewoche-osnabrueck.de/) in August.
Our events are also going to be annouced here on our website a little bit earlier.

Do you want to help shaping the programme of Digitale Woche? Drop by at one of our open chaos meetings or become an organizer yourself!
