Test your knowledge about the internet with the surfschein quiz by internet ABC — get your "drivers license for the internet" at digital week 2025!

The surfschein is a playful digital learning quiz. It provides a protected environment for kids, ages 5 to 12,
to learn the basic skills for responsible and self determined interaction with the internet.

Because the quiz is not translated, this workshop will be held in German only.
