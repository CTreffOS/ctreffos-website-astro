The [Chaos Computer Club (CCC)](https://ccc.de/en) invites all interested parties to join us for the 39th edition of the Chaos Communication Congress under the motto ‘Power Cycles’.
The [39C3](https://events.ccc.de/congress/2025/infos/) will open its doors on 27 December 2025 at the CCH in the heart of Hamburg.

We're going too, with about 15 people and our [own assembly](https://events.ccc.de/congress/2025/hub/de/assembly/detail/ctos).
You can find us in the [Chaos West habitat](https://events.ccc.de/congress/2025/hub/de/assembly/detail/chaoswest).

If you're there, come by and say hi!
