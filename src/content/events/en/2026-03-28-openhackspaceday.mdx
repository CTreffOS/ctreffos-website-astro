On Saturday, March 28th, hackspaces all over Germany and beyond are opening their doors to to the public.
We invite you to visit our Hackspace Rabbithole from 13:37. Come by!

At the same time, we are taking part in the 4th [DI.DAY](/en/diday), which would otherwise have taken place on Easter.

Feel free to drop by with your questions about digital independence. We’ll share advice based on our own experience as well as the collected "recipes" from this nationwide initiative - offering practical help for self-help.
