## Can I bring my problems?

Generally yes, we are happy to help, but we cannot promise that someone will take over your problem or solve it. We are not a IT service provider.

## What is a Chaostreff, are you not the Chaos Computer Club?

No, we are a regional organization. We are not part of the CCC.
However, we often work together with the CCC, e.g. to organise Events or Workshops.
More information can be found on the [Chaos Computer Club](https://www.ccc.de/en/regional).
