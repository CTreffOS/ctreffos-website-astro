# Digital Independence Day

On 27 December 2025, Marc-Uwe Kling presented the idea of the Digital Independence Day
(DI.DAY for short) at the 39th Chaos Communication Congress (39C3) and called on everyone
to get involved.
Regional CCC groups, including Chaostreff Osnabrück, had already prepared events and
supported the call from the stage at the end of the reading.

The idea spread quickly: more groups joined in. CCC Flensburg set up a platform for
managing dates at https://termine.di.day. Since 4 January 2026, the Digital Independence
Day has been taking place on the first Sunday of every month in many cities across Germany
and beyond.

The full reading is available on media.ccc.de in [German](https://media.ccc.de/v/39c3-die-kanguru-rebellion-digital-independence-day) and with an [English live translation](https://media.ccc.de/v/39c3-die-kanguru-rebellion-digital-independence-day#l=eng).

## The Challenge

Large technology corporations now control significant parts of our digital lives:

* They collect vast amounts of data about us
* Their algorithms shape our perception of the world
* They have enormous power over our lives

This concentration of power is a problem – for our privacy, for democracy, and for a free internet.

## What Can We Do?

Better laws and regulation are urgently needed – but waiting for them is not enough. The good
news: we can all act today. For almost every digital service, there are free and open
alternatives.

Here are some examples of areas where switching is possible:

* **Search engines** – privacy-friendly, without tracking
* **Messengers** – with end-to-end encryption
* **Email**, **cloud storage**, **video conferencing**, **browsers**
* **Shopping** – there are countless smaller and larger online shops beyond the big players

## Digital Independence Day in Osnabrück

DI.DAY takes place **every first Sunday of the month** at Chaostreff Osnabrück. It is not
a lecture or a structured course – it is an open get-together where you bring your questions
and we figure out together what works for you.

**What to expect:**

* Bring your questions about digital independence and privacy
* We share tips from our own experience and discuss alternatives with you
* We meet you where you are – every small step counts

**What we can help with:**

Anything related to digital services and software: What alternatives are there? How do you
make the switch? What is more secure, what is easier to use? Feel free to bring your device.

**What we don't help with:**

Hardware repairs and general computer support are not our focus –
there are other places to go for that.

All events in Osnabrück can be found under [Events](/en/#events),
all DI.DAY events at [termine.di.day](https://termine.di.day).
